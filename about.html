Our History

Since we opened in 2008, Baobab has grown to twice the original size and created one of the largest outdoor dining spaces in Wellington. It has become a favourite local hub for the community, a place to relax & forget about how busy the outside world can be, while sipping on a premium blend of Ethiopian espresso or a pint of local boutique craft beer in our tranquil courtyard.
